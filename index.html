About Me

Hi, I’m Alita. I am passionate about bioinformatics and the power of data to make a difference in healthcare. I enjoy the creativity behind developing algorithms, where problem-solving meets innovation, and I find it exciting to use computational methods to tackle real-world challenges. What motivates me most is applying these skills to medical problems, where data-driven insights can directly improve people’s lives. This website introduces who I am, my CV, and some of my past projects.
